Major Map Initiative

The prerequisite mapper lists all the prerequisites for a given class in an intuitive, interactive diagram.

Check out the tutorial below the prerequisite mapper to understand how to use it.

Note: this applet is under development and may have bugs or slightly off prerequisites for certain courses. If you notice anything off please let us know.

Tutorial

Note: Examples used in the tutorial may be inaccurate for the sake of simplification and/or due to updated course catalogs.

Hover on a course node to display the title.
Click on a course node to show its description.

There may be additional details in between asterisks at the end of the description.

Maps may generate messily.

Drag nodes around to rearrange however you like.

Arrow colors indicate groupings.

-Arrows of the same color mean that either one satisfies the course they are pointing to.
- Black arrows indicate that there are no alternatives for that prerequisite.
- In this example, either COGS 109 or COGS 108 (green arrows) can be taken before COGS 118A. Both Math 20E and Math are required.
